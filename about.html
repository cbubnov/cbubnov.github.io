ABOUT


I was born in New Jersey after my parents immigrated to the US from the former USSR. I now reside in Boston, studying at Northeastern University and working at Tulip. I sleep on a mattress on the floor, which bothers people for some reason.

I like to make things better by focusing on all things design, supplemented with communication, front-end code, prototyping, copywriting, user testing, and photography.

You can find my most recent dabblings here.

Quirks: liking when people take pictures of me, being sarcastic, nearly getting lost on hikes in the dark, thinking cats like me even when they strut away.

Whether it's for wandering or whiteboarding, let's chat.
